Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2024

Introduction

Iceberg Theories ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Our app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Theories you have viewed
  • Theories you have marked as favorites
  • Your subscription status
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Analytics and Advertising

We use third-party services (such as TikTok for Business) to analyze app usage and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as IDFA, when permitted)
  • App usage data (such as app launches and in-app events)
  • Purchase events

This data is collected in accordance with the privacy policies of these third-party services. You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings.

Our app uses Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases. These transactions are handled entirely by Apple according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms. If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains content that may not be suitable for young children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@icebergtheories.com

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can reset your local app data at any time through the app's Settings menu by selecting "Reset Progress."
